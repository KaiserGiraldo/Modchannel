About

What is this site?

Modchan is an imageboard based on the idea of making a network where gamers and modders could share their ideas across the platforms. At the same time, it also can be characterized as an internet exchange website where people from all parts can share their thoughts and ideas across the different boards that the site contains.

Please see the official Contact page.


What filetypes are supported here?

txt, jpeg, jpg, bmp, gif, png, ogg, webm, mp4 with more to come.
